About ProjectFunder

ProjectFunder is a platform designed for tertiary‑level students to showcase their academic or innovation projects and receive funding support from individuals, organisations, or alumni. This website empowers students to bring their ideas to life by connecting them with funders who believe in education, creativity, and impact. We proudly align with SDG 4: Quality Education to foster skills, knowledge, and opportunities for all.
